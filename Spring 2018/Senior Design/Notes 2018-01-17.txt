Quadratic Solver Story Examples

Actors: Engineer and administrator who is installing
Quadratic formula: ax^2 + bx + c = 0

Story: Engineering input a,b,c in command line in decimal format.
Story: Formatting input prompts.

Story: Actors do _____ and the result is ______.
Ex:    Engineer uses program and menus are displayed.

Stories are used to communicate between the team and the client.
Tasks (task cards) are things that the team will try to implement between meetings.
    Can include items such as spikes, research, etc.
