Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres,
and today hanfs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits by the Riviere family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musee du Louvre, Paris
