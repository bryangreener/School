	\[ N_x = \mathrm{det}\begin{bmatrix}[rr]8&5\\1&-2\\\end{bmatrix} = -21, \quad N_y = \mathrm{det}\begin{bmatrix}[rr]3&8\\4&1\\\end{bmatrix} = -29 \]
